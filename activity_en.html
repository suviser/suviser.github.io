Activity

This direction is aimed at simplifying the everyday existence of a person, his work, and his personal life. By increasing productivity, reducing its burdensomeness, simplifying and easing life, it contributes to maintaining health and obtaining pleasure.

Projects of "Activity"

"Super Organizer" — an innovative, integrated, cross-platform software aimed at complete subordination and organization of human activities as a whole: both work and leisure. This occurs through maximum simultaneous simplicity and completeness, as they say: "get exactly as many 'hands' as you need" — through integration with hardware (phone, PC), which allows recording, planning, and controlling all one’s activities from real to digital, as well as through the use of modern technologies, such as AI, and future technologies (for example, augmented reality glasses, which, by the way, is also a project of et Human).

"Mobility" (mobile activity) — serious software that serves dual purposes, which can be better described as follows:

"The future department of the company will be engaged in the development of software, operating systems, launchers, and other computer and mobile programs in the broadest sense to enhance the effectiveness of the military (primarily soldiers), as well as the military machine through (specifically) the creation of an information integrative (integrative) military database and cloud with appropriate cyber protection and the creation of new software and/or the acquisition and/or integration of already existing software, which will adapt and otherwise modify and customize mobile devices (smartphones primarily) and other accessories and devices "Inaut"* to suit military needs for the ability to use these devices outside and during combat actions."

* "Inaut" – input/output, meaning accessories that can somehow be connected to a smartphone (or another mobile device like a smartphone) to either receive and store, or transmit data.

— et Human
