Triad of Knowledge

«Triad of Knowledge» — is a code name that encrypts a complex of three programs (applications). The combination of these programs works on the same principle as the human brain. Just as the human mind follows a path in the pursuit of creating knowledge, so does a person through these programs, facilitating each stage of creation.

1 — et Nota

It allows for precise, atomic collection of knowledge. Randomly creating an environment for reflection and recording these thoughts mobilly, it enables the collection of "experience," "data," etc., in an informal form, and as the volume of material becomes infinitely large, it allows for finding "gold" in the "dirt." This is a to-do project, so information about it can be found at Continue... (et Nota)

2 — Clean Sheet (Net Leaf), or Tabula Rasa

Next, the Clean Sheet "stitches" these pieces of knowledge together using a "graph," or "second brain" (as this is somewhat realized in Obsidian). Thus, atoms are combined into molecules, and those, in turn, into cells, and so on, until complete "knowledge" is formed, which then goes on to "publication" or practical application.

(Additionally, the Clean Sheet can be used in the B2B segment for entire companies and corporations. It will have a corporate version, which will be used by almost all employees of the company to accumulate internal "knowledge").

3 — Following (Following + Reporting), or Investigation

And the last tool in the «Triad» Investigation allows conducting research and, so to speak, "de jure" formalize "knowledge" in the form of a "report," which can be an article, a thesis, a book, or a comprehensive life work of a scholar. On one hand, it is the "documentary" presentation of the research, while on the other, it is the final summary that can be published in a journal or book publishing house.

(Moreover, Following can be used in government structures, specifically in the ministries of internal affairs of countries, as it also helps conduct investigations, meaning it documents the process).
